Brand & icon

The mark is a swap of two arrows whose inner arms merge into one unbroken diagonal — a single fluid hand-off from Instacart to Costco Same-Day. It holds from 128 px down to a 16 px favicon.

The merged swap

Top arrow points to Same-Day; bottom arrow returns. Their inner arms share one diagonal stroke through the center — the “transfer” made literal. Stroke weight scales up at small sizes to stay legible.

Space, radius & elevation

A tight 380 px popup needs a disciplined spacing ramp. Radii nest by two — outer shell 13, cards 11, controls 10. Elevation is sparing: one resting shadow, one for the floating popup, one warm glow under the primary action.
